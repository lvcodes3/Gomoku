Help (instructions)

  1. Your main goal is to get 5 in a row, whether it's horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

  2. Your opponent has the same goal, who ever accomplishes this first, wins the game.

  3. Unlimited time, until someone wins or the game board fills up completely.

  4. Player 1 (person who is logged in) always goes first, Player 2 must go second.
